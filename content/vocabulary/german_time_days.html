💡 Important Notes

  • All days of the week are masculine and use "der"
  • "halb" (half) refers to half past the previous hour: "halb vier" = 3:30
  • German uses 24-hour format officially: 15:30 = drei Uhr dreißig
  • "Uhr" is used after numbers when telling time: "drei Uhr" (3 o'clock)
  • "nach" = past, "vor" = to/before
  • "Viertel" = quarter (15 minutes)
  • Time expressions can be formal (Es ist dreizehn Uhr) or informal (Es ist eins)
