🇩🇪 German W-Words Complete Guide

Master all essential W-words for questions, directions, time, and relationships

❓Question Words (Fragewörter)
WER
WHO (person - subject)
[vehr]
Wer ist das? - Who is that?
💡 WER = WHO (both ask about people)
WEN
WHO/WHOM (person - accusative object)
[vehn]
Wen siehst du? - Who(m) do you see?
💡 WEN = WHOM (accusative case)
WEM
WHO/WHOM (person - dative object)
[vehm]
Wem hilfst du? - Who are you helping?
💡 WEM = TO WHOM (dative case)
WESSEN
WHOSE (possession)
[VEH-sen]
Wessen Auto ist das? - Whose car is that?
💡 WESSEN = WHOSE (genitive case)
WAS
WHAT (thing)
[vas]
Was machst du? - What are you doing?
💡 WAS = WHAT (both start with W)
WELCH-
WHICH (selection)
[velsh]
Welches Buch? - Which book?
💡 Changes ending: welcher/welche/welches
WIE
HOW
[vee]
Wie geht's? - How are you?
💡 WIE = HOW (sounds like "we")
WIE VIEL
HOW MUCH
[vee feel]
Wie viel kostet das? - How much does that cost?
💡 WIE VIEL = HOW MUCH (quantity)
WIE VIELE
HOW MANY
[vee FEE-leh]
Wie viele Bücher? - How many books?
💡 WIE VIELE = HOW MANY (countable)
WARUM
WHY
[va-ROOM]
Warum fragst du? - Why do you ask?
💡 WARUM = WHY (reason)
WIESO
WHY/HOW COME
[vee-ZOH]
Wieso nicht? - Why not?
💡 WIESO = WHY (informal)
WESHALB
WHY/FOR WHAT REASON
[VES-halp]
Weshalb kommst du so spät? - Why are you so late?
💡 WESHALB = WHY (formal reason)
📍Place & Direction (Ort & Richtung)
WO
WHERE (location - static)
[voh]
Wo wohnst du? - Where do you live?
💡 WO = WHERE (static location)
WOHIN
WHERE TO (direction - going to)
[vo-HIN]
Wohin gehst du? - Where are you going?
💡 WO + HIN = WHERE + TO
WOHER
WHERE FROM (origin - coming from)
[vo-HEHR]
Woher kommst du? - Where are you from?
💡 WO + HER = WHERE + FROM
WODURCH
THROUGH WHAT/WHEREBY
[vo-DOORKH]
Wodurch hast du das gelernt? - How did you learn that?
💡 WO + DURCH = WHERE + THROUGH
WORAUF
ON WHAT/WHAT...ON
[vo-ROWF]
Worauf wartest du? - What are you waiting for?
💡 WO + AUF = WHERE + ON
WORIN
IN WHAT/WHEREIN
[vo-RIN]
Worin besteht der Unterschied? - What's the difference?
💡 WO + IN = WHERE + IN
WOBEI
WHEREBY/WHILE DOING
[vo-BYE]
Wobei kann ich helfen? - How can I help?
💡 WO + BEI = WHERE + AT/WHILE
⏰Time Words (Zeitwörter)
WANN
WHEN (specific time)
[van]
Wann kommst du? - When are you coming?
💡 WANN = WHEN (double N for specific time)
WENN
WHEN/IF (conditional, repeated)
[ven]
Wenn ich Zeit habe... - When/If I have time...
💡 WENN = IF/WHEN (conditions, habits)
WIE LANGE
HOW LONG (duration)
[vee LAN-geh]
Wie lange dauert das? - How long does that take?
💡 WIE LANGE = HOW LONG (time duration)
WIE OFT
HOW OFTEN (frequency)
[vee oft]
Wie oft gehst du ins Kino? - How often do you go to the cinema?
💡 WIE OFT = HOW OFTEN (frequency)
WIE SPÄT
WHAT TIME (clock time)
[vee shpayt]
Wie spät ist es? - What time is it?
💡 WIE SPÄT = WHAT TIME (clock)
WOCHENTAG
WHAT DAY (day of week)
[VO-khen-tahk]
Welcher Wochentag ist heute? - What day is today?
💡 WOCHEN + TAG = WEEK + DAY
🔗Relative Pronouns (Relativpronomen)
WAS (relative)
THAT/WHICH (refers to whole clause)
[vas]
Das, was er sagt... - That which he says...
💡 WAS as relative pronoun for neuter/unknown
WO (relative)
WHERE (relative location)
[voh]
Die Stadt, wo ich wohne... - The city where I live...
💡 WO in relative clauses = WHERE
WOZU
FOR WHAT PURPOSE/WHY
[vo-TSOO]
Wozu brauchst du das? - What do you need that for?
💡 WO + ZU = WHERE + TO (purpose)
WOMIT
WITH WHAT
[vo-MIT]
Womit schreibst du? - What are you writing with?
💡 WO + MIT = WHERE + WITH
WOVON
OF WHAT/FROM WHAT
[vo-FON]
Wovon sprichst du? - What are you talking about?
💡 WO + VON = WHERE + FROM/OF
WOFÜR
FOR WHAT
[vo-FUER]
Wofür ist das gut? - What is that good for?
💡 WO + FÜR = WHERE + FOR
💫Other Important W-Words
WÄHREND
DURING/WHILE
[VAY-rent]
Während des Essens... - During the meal...
💡 WÄHREND = DURING (temporal)
WEGEN
BECAUSE OF/DUE TO
[VAY-gen]
Wegen des Regens... - Because of the rain...
💡 WEGEN = BECAUSE OF (reason)
WIE
LIKE/AS (comparison)
[vee]
So groß wie ich - As big as me
💡 WIE = LIKE/AS (comparisons)
WOMÖGLICH
POSSIBLY/PERHAPS
[vo-MÖGH-likh]
Womöglich regnet es. - It might rain.
💡 WO + MÖGLICH = WHERE + POSSIBLE
WEDER
NEITHER
[VAY-der]
Weder... noch... - Neither... nor...
💡 WEDER...NOCH = NEITHER...NOR
WEITER
FURTHER/CONTINUE
[VY-ter]
Weiter so! - Keep it up!
💡 WEITER = FURTHER (continuation)
WIEDER
AGAIN
[VEE-der]
Bis morgen wieder! - See you again tomorrow!
💡 WIEDER = AGAIN (repetition)
WICHTIG
IMPORTANT
[VIKH-tikh]
Das ist wichtig. - That is important.
💡 WICHTIG = IMPORTANT (significance)
