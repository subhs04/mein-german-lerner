🔊 German Pronunciation Guide

ä
like "a" in "cat" but more open
FAEH, LAEF, SHLAEF
ö
like "ur" in "fur" but rounder
(not used in these verbs)
ü
like "oo" but with rounded lips
(not used in these verbs)
au
like "ow" in "cow"
LOW
äu
like "oy" in "boy"
LOY
ie
like "ee" in "see"
LEES, ZEES
ch
like clearing your throat gently
SHPREH-khen
sch
like "sh" in "ship"
SHLAH, SHPREH
💡 Capital letters indicate stressed syllables
